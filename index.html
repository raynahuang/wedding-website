Our Love Story
from a message to forever
♡

It started with a message, quiet and simple, but somehow it felt like something already written.

In January 2024, Rayna and Sam found each other online. From Taiwan and Calgary, their paths crossed in Vancouver, as if distance had only been part of the plan.

What began as a conversation became a rhythm — days filled with words, laughter, and a growing sense that this was not by chance. After two weeks, they began dating. Naturally, effortlessly.

Rayna was drawn to the way Sam speaks about what's ahead — steady, certain, with a light in his eyes. Sam found in Rayna something rare: the same values, the same way of seeing the world.

By February 28, 2024, what had been quietly unfolding became a relationship.

Since then, they have travelled across places and moments, discovering not just new cities, but new pieces of each other. They met each other's families, shared cultures, and built a story in between.

On November 21, 2025, Sam asked. And she said yes — like the answer had always been there, waiting.
